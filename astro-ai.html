Astro AI - Machine Learning

Stellar parameter predictions using supervised learning. I worked as an undergraduate researcher for the summer of 2023 in Kwang Moo Yi's Computer Vision Lab at the University of British Columbia (UBC).

Developed ML models and frameworks to process spectra data from the ESA's Gaia telescope. Utilized a large astronomy dataset and HPC clusters to accelerate and scale the data processing. Collaborated closely with a team of experts, presenting progress updates and proposing new ideas. Contributed to a cutting-edge software pipeline for spectra-to-stellar parameters in astrophysics, achieving a more complete map of chemical evolution in the Milky Way galaxy.
